Our Services

How can Messaround Records help you?

Production Management

Messaround offers full service for your production needs. We will coordinate recording sessions, mixing and mastering, photo and video shoots, and release timelines so that you can focus on your music.

Graphic Design

Your album needs the right art to pop. Messaround takes care of the design of CD covers and coordinates printing and packaging of CDs and download cards.

Web Design

You are only as known as your brand, and for that you need a solid online presence. We can build and maintain your very own artist website where your fans can find out news about your music and concerts.

Digital Distribution

Get your music out there! Sell your releases directly to your fans with Bandcamp, or syndicate to iTunes, Spotify, Amazon Music and more with TuneCore. We deal with the hassle so you don’t have to.

Social Media

This is probably your most important marketing tool at the moment. Messaraound can help you get the word out on social media with Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube.

Royalties Administration

You got into it for the music, not the administration. We can register your original music with performing rights agencies, and administer worldwide royalties for performances and for original music publication.

Our Story

Messaround Records was launched in 2018 by Mikey Ariel, a.k.a. Miss Mikey May. While recording her own album, she realized that not only is she capable of managing the production and distribution of her own music, she enjoys it!

And if she can do it for herself, she can do it for other musicians.

So Messaround Records was born. Our approach is different, but simple: We work for you, not you for us. A traditional record label normally signs an artist, pays for the entire production and distribution, and pays out royalties and advances. Messaround Records offers services at a flat fee or sales percentage. We believe this gives you, the artist, more freedom and independence in creating and publishing your music.

Our job is to set up the administrative and business infrastructure so that our artists can focus on creating and performing their music. We cater to local and regional artists who might just need that little extra help to get their music recorded and published.

Come join the Messaround family!
